DIETDesign Impact Evaluation Tactic

DIET has 3 stages

  1. 1. StrategyWhen you first hear about the project
  2. 2. ProblemOnce you have completed your research
  3. 3. SolutionWhen you have designed and tested

What is DIET?

DIET helps teams in predicting the chance of success of a project or retrospectively see why it went well, or didn't.
