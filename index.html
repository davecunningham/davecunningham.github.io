My Phylosophy

As a web designer putting together a wireframe you are looking for potential problems and how the page will be used with the content the client has. You are trying to break the page and throw every scenario you can possibly think of. You are also thinking of how the content will work across a multitude of devices along with all the different people with very different needs that will be looking at the page.
